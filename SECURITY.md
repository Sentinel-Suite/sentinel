# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in Sentinel Suite, please report it responsibly.

**Email:** security@sentinel.local

### What to Include

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if any)

### Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgment:** Within 48 hours
- **Assessment:** Within 5 business days
- **Resolution:** Based on severity, typically within 30 days

### Responsible Disclosure

We ask that you:

1. Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have addressed it
2. Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond what is necessary to demonstrate it
3. Do not access or modify data belonging to other users

We will credit reporters who follow responsible disclosure guidelines (unless anonymity is preferred).
